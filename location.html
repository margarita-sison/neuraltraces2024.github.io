Location

The Physikalisch-Technische Bundesanstalt (PTB) is the national metrology institute in Germany. It is located in Charlottenburg, Berlin, close to the Zoologischer Garten and Ernst-Reuter-Platz stations.

Getting to PTB in Berlin-Charlottenburg

Public transportation to PTB's Berlin Institute

  • • Bus M45, Otto-Suhr-Allee bus stop
  • • 245, Marchstraße bus stop
  • • Bus 101, Cauerstraße/Guerickestraße bus stop
  • • U-Bahn (underground): Ernst-Reuter-Platz Station
  • • S-Bahn (commuter train): Zoologischer Garten or Tiergarten Stations

From BER Airport to PTB’s Berlin Institute

  • • S9 in the direction to Spandau Bhf to S+U Zoologischer Garten and then take the U2 in the direction to Theodor-Heuss-Platz and get off at Ernst-Reuter-Platz

From Berlin Central Railway Station (Berlin Hauptbahnhof) to PTB's Berlin Institute

Leave the station at the exit to Europaplatz, Invalidenstraße

  • • Bus 245 in the direction to Zoologischer Garten, get off at Dovebrücke bus stop. Walk the way the bus was going as far as Abbestraße (about 100 m), after about 50 m you will see the entrance to PTB on the left-hand side of the street. Or by S-Bahn from platform 16 with the lines:
  • → S3 in the direction to Spandau Bhf
  • → S5 in the direction to Westkreuz
  • → S7 in the direction to Potsdam Hauptbahnhof
  • → S9 in the direction to Spandau Bhf
