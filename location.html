PTB is the national metrology institute in Germany. It is located in Charlottenburg, Berlin, close to the Ernst-Reuter-Platz metro station and the Zoologischer Garten regional train station.

Getting to PTB in Berlin-Charlottenburg

1. By public transportation

Take any of the following connections:
    • Bus line M45, Otto-Suhr-Allee bus stop
  • • Bus line 245, Marchstraße bus stop
  • • Bus line 101, Cauerstraße/Guerickestraße bus stop
  • • U-Bahn (underground) lines U2, U9: Ernst-Reuter-Platz or Zoologischer Garten stations
  • • S-Bahn (commuter train) lines S3, S5, S7, S9: Zoologischer Garten or Tiergarten stations

2. Coming from BER Airport

Take the S-Bahn line S9 in the direction to Spandau Bhf. Get off at S+U Zoologischer Garten and then take the U2 in the direction to Theodor-Heuss-Platz and get off at Ernst-Reuter-Platz.

3. Coming from Berlin Central Station (Berlin Hauptbahnhof)

  • • Leave the station at the exit Europaplatz, Invalidenstraße. Take bus line 245 in the direction to Zoologischer Garten, get off at Dovebrücke bus stop.
  • • Or, take any of the following S-Bahn lines from platform 16: S3 in the direction to Spandau Bhf, S5 in the direction to Westkreuz, S7 in the direction to Potsdam Hauptbahnhof, S9 in the direction to Spandau Bhf

Find the lecture hall on PTB campus

Enter the southern campus part from Abbestraße and head towards the Herrmann-von-Helmholtz-Bau (see campus map). Inside the Helmholtz-Bau, directions to the lecture hall will be provided.
