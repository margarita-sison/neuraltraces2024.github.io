Advanced M/EEG Methods
and Clinical Applications
Workshop

  • Contact

    connect@neuraltraces2024.org

  • Dates

    10-12th April, 2024

  • Location

    PTB, Berlin


The workshop will cover advanced data analysis methods and their clinical applications in equal measure. Methodological sessions will cover signal processing and time series modeling, forward and inverse modeling, machine learning and multivariate modeling, as well as contributions to open science. Clinical sessions will cover the application of these M/EEG methods across various clinical domains, including movement disorders, aging and dementias, psychiatric and developmental disorders, and epilepsy. Sessions will include invited as well as contributed talks. In addition, a poster session will be held.
